Khabib to Conor: "It is the same thing"

It's the same thing as the previous fight. If it's a first round finish, I'd back Conor. From the second and further, then Dustin wins.

He memorably scaled the Octagon at UFC 229 after dafeating McGregor to attack his cornerman Dillon Danis, who had reportedly shouted insults at Khabib following the fight. The Russian has turned to promoting following his retirement and is mentoring.
